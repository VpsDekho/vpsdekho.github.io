For outbound traffic, inbound traffic, and the total bandwidth usage, how is it calculated?
The costs are calculated based on outgoing traffic only, not inbound traffic. For example, if your monthly bandwidth quota is 4TB, it is calculated as follows: 4TB/672hr(24hr*28days)=5.95GB/hr.
